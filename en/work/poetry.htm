Poetry

I have been writing poetry on and off for ten years now, and I am pleased to offer this collection of my poems for the public to enjoy. These poems all mean something to me, and I hope that others will find their own meanings and things to appreciate in my words. For the past year I have been writing much more frequently as I try to take my poetry more seriously, and I hope that I will have many more poems to add to this collection in the years to come.
