Expanding Foam

Foam expands to fill the cracks
In the tree trunk hollow,
Where the cosy shadows and fallen leaves
Cannot hide the fact of life
From portentous peering eyes;

A bit flips on a motherboard and soon a van arrives,
The tree buzzes with bees now parted from their hive;
A pair of birds scream for their children trapped inside

And the next day sit silent, warm
In the summer hanging air
The bees are gone, a complaint resolved,

They wreak death without thought —
It’s a winning formula
