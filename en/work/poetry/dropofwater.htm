Drop of Water

A little sphere sinks into the surface
And, disappearing from view
Sends concentric waves in all directions
As a shaky pillar rises from the void

Surrounded by a baubled coronet
And, growing thinner and higher
Releases a smaller ball in the air
To start the process anew
