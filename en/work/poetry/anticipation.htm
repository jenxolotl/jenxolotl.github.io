Anticipation

Desire for tomorrow tightens my chest
Quickens my pulse and makes thoughts
Race with a burning hot energy
Tempered by emerging images

The expectations of words and action
Giving feeling itself creating feeling
A glowing feedback loop of ecstasy
Enabled in the delay, in the unknowing

Rise of my passions to the wait
The cup taking shape in my thirst
My anticipation for the sun to set
And my night to become

But the day beyond is not what I crave
But the promise of another tomorrow
The fire always burning in my heart
An enduring power of my mind

To draw pictures in the sand
