Encounter

As the din quietens
As the darkness falls
In the rising streetlamp glow
Down the same path

Down the same long road
Into the depths of your eyes
The peaks of your laughter
The troughs of curbside gutters

To the same place
To the same soft bed
Under the weight of your body
Under the weight of your lips

In the silence of the night
Without expectation
Without hesitation
The walls falling like sand

Between my fingers
Between the threads of your hair
Against the roundness of your breasts
Against the line of your jaw

Time stops.

Without stopping
The chirping of the birds
The first peek of light
The start of car engines

To the heartbeat rising again
To the return of normality
To the parting of ways
The creation of memories

In the crowded trains
In the office cubicles
On the factory floors
On the face of the Moon,

Still shining brightly
