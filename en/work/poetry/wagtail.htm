Willy Wagtail

Little willy wagtail, you broke my concentration!

Swinging on the fence while
Looking side to side,
With sharp streaks above your little eyes.

A graceful concatenation
Of form and feel and function,
You swing your little head about

To meet setting Sun,
And moths, and flies
With a dip and a flap

You fall into space
Like it's nothing, a test run
And then you dart!

And catch one,
And smack it on the pavers,
Tap tap tap

The cutest sound of a killer
I have ever heard, and seen
You chase much bigger birds

You, my friend of the garden,
Inspector of my Fence, with
Moves as deadly as you look —

Always a consummate professional.
