TDOV

The tangled threads of existence
Floating like gossamers
In invisible currents

Collide into corporeal forms
To take on their shape
And steal their flesh

And mould the spirit of life
Anew in tossed aside bits of carbon
Tangled structures arising from void

In emergent patterns of light and sound
Borrowed from nature, borrowed
From fellow man

And heaved up to hurl back
Into the freebase semiotics
Of the tears in jeans and yellow socks

Worn razor thin — I make holes in everything

Through to beautiful chaos primordial
Soaring back through the air
Or killed dead on the rocks
