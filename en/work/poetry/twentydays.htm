Twenty Days

Twenty days I have waited for this
And learned how to wait, and to not wait
But to live in the occasion
Travelling from end to end across the Noongar lands
Biding my time without biding,

Making the moment worthwhile,
In the little things, the picnic spots
And chance encounters that make
Every day interesting, building

A foundation to be ready for
This day, the distant goal come home
And found to be there all along
I was never alone but had you there

With me always, the thought of you
Soon to be subsumed by the real again
Like sand washing away on the beach;
I will etch out a new image

And give it to you as a gift.
