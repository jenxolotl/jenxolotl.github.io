Spring Glow

The spots of white clouds spattered across the blue sky
In twos with the people frolicking everywhere before the rolling fountains
On green lawns like in a Renaissance painting

Made a movie scene backdrop of our first kiss, perfectly illuminated,
The Sun making a halo through your wavy strands of hair,
The light in your eyes, on your gently smiling face
Warm and softly glowing in the reflection of my image

Coming back to you, I realized the perfect time had come and asked the question
Your lips moving in to answer saw and rejected the comparison
Even a dream, another thought of mine, is not alike enough
And the comfortable warmth, the things I remember,

The way your mouth neatly found mine, the dainty size of them like rose petals
And contour smooth and sweet like crème brûlée
Are only analogues for what I felt,
Holding your bottom lip with mine, the incredible softness

I saw, the black wispy peach fuzz I adored and bronze shine of the sunscreen off your shoulder
Even in the moment I knew it would never be repeated,
To savour the uncatchable as much as I could, in reality
The feeling of our connection

Simply indescribable
