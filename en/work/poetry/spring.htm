Spring

Surrounded by the sounds of spring
The chitters and twills of birds all around
Their shadows passing over the trees
Bedecked with golden green leaves

The figures of soaring ibises
And the rustles of finches
Flitting through the litter
Beneath the flowering bushes

Everything is alive and moving
In a shimmering panorama
Of song, dance and colour;
In this moment

Even the constant drone
Of trucks does not disturb me
A dragonfly and V formation
Go drifting past
