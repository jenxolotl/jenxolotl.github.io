Long Weekend

The weekend not over the weight
Of the world lies heavy
On my eyelids, and I am tempted
By everything, from drink to women

To a newly discovered religious fanaticism
Not to lay down and accept my fate;
The child in the mirror laughs
At my irresponsibility.
