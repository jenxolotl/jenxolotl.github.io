Dusk

The caw of crows growing
Low, lower, and long drawn
Out cry from the top of the tree
Reminds me of days spent home

After school, how long did I stay

Before I was born, these hands
Would have toiled for potatoes
While the ink dried slow on the page
And the wait came to be expected.
