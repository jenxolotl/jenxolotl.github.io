Reflections on a Car Door

Looking at the distorted shape of cars
Twisting round the bend of contours
In red sedan doors, waiting at the light

On the way to work, the Sun
Blasting beams of white heat
Through my windshield,
Blinding my eyes, the spots

Lingering in the corners
Of my vision, the dots
That compose my inkjet reality,
The things I focus my gaze on

To remind me of my subjectivity;
To keep feeding my own vanity
