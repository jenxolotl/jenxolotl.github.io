Anticipation (II)

The energy builds like in a looming rainstorm:
The humidity rises, the clouds gather
The air is heavy making me break into cold sweat

I feel the static charge in the tips of my fingers
My heart tightens in growing excitement

I know what to expect now
The feel of your touch the look in your eyes
The sweet smell of the earth like oozing chocolate
The release of being properly drenched in the rain

People say it is reckless to be out in the open
Against a coming storm
But I would rather risk electrocution
Than not feel it over and over;

This weather reminds me of Newman
