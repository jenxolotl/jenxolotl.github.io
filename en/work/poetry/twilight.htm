Twilight

Night trees and orange sky
The atmosphere glows like fire
As it approaches the celestial limit.

From violet to orange to glorious yellow
The light floods in and the stars recede
Back beyond the dense colour

That returns to everything
People, places, liminal spaces
The earth rich and full of life

The pink on my cheeks
Matches my finest jacket —
I see clearly beyond

Marching to the rising sun.

The stars in their delicate beauty
Adorning the Moon
That guided me through the formless grey

Of a much smaller world and
The sweet suffocating mist
That hides behind the thinnest of screens.
