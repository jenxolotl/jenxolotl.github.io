Violation

The breath on their lips
Sinks into my skin
And draws deeply
From my veins

Leaving stark patches
Like dead grass
Where there is no rain.

Places once soft and sweet
Become dry and sharp
Like razors
Slicing into passing fingers

The smell lingers long
After the frenzied eyes
And lunatic grins
Fade into the drowning darkness

That consumes all colour
Within.

—

The water will come
But the feeling always
Remains
