On the Limits of Language

Oppress me
In mundane salience
The track of timeworn gradients
Made tired and shallow
Like the sike
By sleepy hollow

Muddled effluvient
The detritus
Of ten thousand years
Ex parte Visuvii cinerum
Fossilized and fragmented
Figments of our imagination

共同幻想
安保大革命
我想说
火星文，但是
Hypoxemic events
In strange grammars

Still follow meekly
Behind the contours
Pada semaya puniki
Equally outside
Many tip-toeing tongues
Myriad dachtyl single meme

Repress me
Harder, Adonai
Reduce me to form
Over function;
Fail to see me under
The curves mens mea
