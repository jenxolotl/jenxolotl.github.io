Wandering (Cape Arid)

Wandering the long sandy track
White dust caking the worn out soles of my shoes
I didn’t expect to find you out here
Descending from fields of flowers
To our little fork in the road

Here the land is dotted with the sparks of red-tipped banksias
And the wind drives crystal blue waves against the rocks
Relentlessly

Overlooking the pristine coastline
Receding like a brushstroke into a single horsehair
How could I miss you, a fine and dainty speck of colour
Against the rugged magnificence of everything

Let’s walk, and walk a little further
Towering clouds shadow the headlands
Rolling past, the land and sea are beautiful
This track still curves around and
I don’t see my turn off, yet.
