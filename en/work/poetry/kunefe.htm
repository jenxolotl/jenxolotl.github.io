Künefe

Out dark in the city night
In the broken shadows
Of undying light
Reaching passing the milling throngs

Outside stone glass storefronts
The sound of subwoofers
Thumping screeching tyre tracks
Left in the mind, beyond

The homeless on cold grey steps
In the seclusion of your presence
The colour bright in muzzled silence
Eating künefe with you

With flimsy wooden spoons
Touching pulling the tangled threads
Flicking the mosquito from my face
When I felt your falling fingers

Against me
The stadium looked so close
