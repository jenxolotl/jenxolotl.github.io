Mother’s Day (Milkshake)

In a break with long established tradition
I invite you out for coffee today, to learn
From the more filial daughter

You are in Bali, as you told me, as I should have known
In a café in Mundaring I am surrounded by mothers
Old and wrinkled and still nursing their babies
Accompanied only by the flavour of a vanilla milkshake
