Kaleidoscope

A kaleidoscope of romantic images
Goes running through my mind
A clumsy pastiche of true love
Run through a coffee ground filter

Of Hollywood movies and hackneyed novels
The overdone plotlines running well within
Hollowed-out contours of other people’s stories;
A jumbled mish-mash of secondhand feelings

That will dissipate
When I hear your voice again
