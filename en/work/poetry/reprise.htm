Reprise

Black cat
Here we are again
Coming back home

From Dwarda way, where
My piss makes the earth
Smell like perfume

On the long road
Back from Mundijong,
The traffic lights distract

Me
From my own
Ego

Small rising coloured orbs
The blossoms of the urban
Age following where I go;

At home, the booms
And sirens still
To my surprise

The show continues
