Texting, friday night

Winding away the small hours talking
With you, sweet ever-distant muse
The feel of your texts warm
Like the blankets crumpled below me

Soft
Like the springs buoying me
Into joyfully recounting
The caves and mountains and past loves

Grand adventures fading out
Wispy clouds in the backdrop
Of your landscape;
Thanks to you I will sleep

Dreaming of that night in Cooloongup
