The Destitute on South Street

The destitute on South Street
A matching husband and wife team
In flannelette shirts
Marching up and down the median strip

With a cardboard sign that reads
“Anything helps”

As I look on
With my leather gloves
On the wheel, their misery is
Only a hair’s width away
