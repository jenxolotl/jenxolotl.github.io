Paganoni

Out in the swamp
I hear the regular roar
Of the hometime trains
Filling the bush
With metallic echoes

The limbs of the rushes
Catch my feet,
The tails of the karraks
Look like crimson fires
In the sky;

The lights
Of the passing cars
Look like meteors
Through the night
