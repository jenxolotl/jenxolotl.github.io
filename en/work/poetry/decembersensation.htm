december sensation

There is still beauty all around,
The christmas trees and jacarandas blooming
Brightly in strange colours, like
Premature festive fireworks raised to mark

The passing of a new era, the petals
Cover the ground in soft textures crushed underfoot,
The feelings drying out too soon
Beneath the unforgiving disc of the sun

And pale blue pall of the sky and sea
Blending together in beautiful subtlety;
The emotions rich and rejuvenated skin
With a thin crust of salt and the oily smear of suncreen

Soft like your memory, fading
Into humid lightning nights and the sound of mole crickets
Digging into the brown grass, tanning
Like my hands into the long dry spells

Away from your touch, your name etched
With burning fingers into bathtub floors circling

Too close to the drain, the christmas trees
Still burn with the flame but will wither
And dry into the long quiet heat,
My lips cracked with the promise

Of rain again, somewhere, sometime,
To fill the earth with beads of sweat
And make it plump with your image,
The flowers returning everywhere and breath sweet

Like scarlet roses
