Indulgence

Cut into me with your sharpened thorns
Dig your nails into my skin, make me bleed
Press rocks into the soles of my feet
Trip me into the hard gravel, bruise me

Make me stumble in the shade of
Your power, my lips cracked with thirst
Beads of sweat pooling down reddened skin
Covered in the dirt and flies and filth

In your presence, broken and aching
I can’t get enough of your
Unyielding disregard for me, you
The most intense of my lovers

Indulge me with your indifference
