Ducks in a Pond

I yearn to see you so damn much,
The desire burning in my heart till it hurts,
I passionately want to be
Consumed completely

By you, to become raw energy
As I wait and my lips crack,
And my belly starves
For just one bite of you

A drop of water in the desert,
As I waste away,
The duck in the pond raises its wings
High over its head and

Beats the waves repeatedly;
You were with me all along
