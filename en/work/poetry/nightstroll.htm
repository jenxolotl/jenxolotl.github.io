Night Stroll (Fremantle)

The moonlight hangs over Joe’s Fish Shack
Draping a silken pall over the faded paint
Shopfront, and the blackened windows
Behind bright orange barriers receded

Into darkness, the life and chatter
And clinks of glasses faded into ghostly
Echo, beyond the silence of the night
And the calm coolness that remains

Distantly aloof from the skin,
Boarded up into trembling hands
And the long winding footpaths
To dead ends, where the rats roam free

And the blackness subsumes all doubt;
The clouds blot out the stars and
The waves ride quietly, imperceptibly
Into the lights of the quay
