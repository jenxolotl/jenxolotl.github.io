Five Days (More)

Five days more to go now
And I will hold you in my arms
Again like I never have and
I always knew beyond the doubt

That it would happen, that
Your eyes, your hands and lips
Would dissolve into me, stirred
By a pool of storming currents

Beneath my outward reason
I must admit I have been
Driven mad by desire
And my days brim

With excitement
