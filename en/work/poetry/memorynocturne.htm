memory (nocturne)

Even now I still think of you

A year gone and
Ten thousand miles away
The locks of curly hair
And round rim glasses
And little dogs and
Nerdy obsessions

All the slightest things remind me of you
Your face ever present in your absence
And crass remarks ringing out clearly
Into the long lonely distance

The passage of time has not dulled
The sting I feel when I remember
The warmth I shared with you
For that moment in the sun

Did you know I really loved you?
And still do, though I ask myself
Whether it was the right thing, if
You really loved me too

If love is not another word
For a misplaced nostalgia
