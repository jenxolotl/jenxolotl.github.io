Old Wheels

I'm getting these old wheels turning
Creaking, groaning
And pulling
The tired rubber treads
Across the cold factory floor

Disturbing the dust laid long before
Which now rises
And fills the stale air

With a million tangled stars
Made luminous
By the moonlight
Shining from a broken window.
