friday catchup (bird)

i thought of offering her a kiss or
simply leaning in a bit more
to see what happens and i strongly suspect
it would have happened but

i didn’t quite reach the moment and
when she told me she didn’t quite feel ready
yet to meet new people and it was about time
she needed to head home i knew

that hesitation was the right choice
tho something was certainly there i knew
she saw in my eyes my own lips perhaps
in the undoing action a treasured friend

i don’t regret it and i don’t regret too
telling her later that i was tempted which
like a kiss is an action that is
safer behind the distance of home the

barrier of a screen
