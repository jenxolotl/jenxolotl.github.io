A Sketch of Wave Rock

Sitting up on Wave Rock
In the shadow of worn out stones
The ambient drone of festival-goers
Below drowned out in the silent

Boyfriend watching calm beside you
Arms rested on knees, pencil steady etching
Image in your book, black streaked rock
Hanging high above the treescape

Perfect line, exquisite shading filled in
Foreground shapes sharp against the backdrop
Of setting sun and sheer granite face behind
With singlet and short shorts above the circus

Field of tents I dared not disturb
You, working your masterpiece
The Humps sitting alone clear over
The sunburnt horizon, beautiful afterimage

I can only hope caught
Similarly in my own reflection
On the page, dried ink
Written toward a particular feeling.
