A Happy Place

The brightest blue upholds the sky
Looking through your eyes
Like a thousand azulejos tiles
Crossing the old church walls

And above the pretty patterns
That hold us all in place
I hear one voice rising clearly
Above the godless din

Like a hundred singing honeybirds
Harmonizing in the thick
Of dense yellow wattle groves
In the jungle dancing all the while

The warming Sun soothes my skin
Your fingers pressing on my arms
Polished nails and cuticles
An unending sense of calm

And your voice goes up
Through all the trees
Past all the squawking Carnaby's
Lying in soft beds

Matted hair and heavy head
Two patches of soft grass
In the coming shadows of the night
By the old tractor at the farm

Blue is all around us
In the big skies above
And it lies in your shining eyes
It runs wet through my veins

It feels cold in the water
Where we dip our toes
It'll sting for a moment
Until we let it go

In the day's dying hours
In your close embrace
Your voice still rings clearly
And I see your face

The blue sky shines bright above
This happy, happy place

The blue sky shines bright above
This happy, happy place
