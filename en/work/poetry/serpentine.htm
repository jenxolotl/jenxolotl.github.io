Serpentine

Life flowing everlasting
In the long hot gaze of summer
Bathe me in your yellow glow

Let me relish in your cries
Clucks, crows, and croons
The powdered light of

A full moon, seen over the dam
Holding so much potential
Within you;

When it all crashes down
I want to be swallowed in your wake
And feel it all

Come rushing back again
