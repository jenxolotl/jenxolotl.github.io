Upselling

You’d be crazy not to sell
Your house, in this market
And buy somewhere bigger
And better in Como,

Or Scarborough, with amenities
And access to the beach
The property valuations show
The profit you stand to make

On what you paid fifteen, ten
Even five years ago
The people talk of places
To buy their dreams

In the restaurants and offices
In one part of Perth;
People talk of rent too high
To afford a single room

On a full-time income
To give up eating, drinking
Hoping, forget hobbies
Family, to survive is enough

In another part,
On the windswept doorsteps
At the traffic lights turned red
The vans in carparks at dawn

Regardless of place
Don’t we all wish
We had bought when
It was cheaper
