Echo (Gooralong Brook #2)

All the beautiful sights, and sounds
Cannot capture through their presence
How I truly feel about someone
A world away

My words are without meaning but to me, myself
Black arm bands, bearded lizards, fields of flowers
The weird foods and funky RPGs

The memories I conjure, and the pictures I can paint
Can only point a line toward
What I really mean to say:

In all of it, in everything,
In the river ruts and summer heat
Through the city streets and outback trips
Between the sunrise and the darkness

You are somehow felt,
The presence in your absence;
Everywhere I am,
The absence of your presence.
