Commute (Monday)

The sunlight crosses the carriages in strange shapes;
Across the aisle, a silhouetted “TAKE A BREAK”

The silence is broken by the faint whine of engines and an old woman coughing
Next station, Cockburn Central

They never pronounce it correctly out of false modesty
The doors screech and people keep staring at their phones, indifferent

A rondel of coughs and sneezes rises and goes up and down the air space
