Nightfall (Petrudor #2)

Lying in the comfort of my swag
I know exactly why I am here
The cool and gentle breeze soothes me
Like your touch against my skin

And the softly repeating croaks
Of the frogs in the still rock ponds
Calm the torrents in my mind
Like the words that leave your lips

The stars emerge all around, shimmering
Beads of glass in the ornamental frame
Of branches all around me, holding me
As closely as when we’re together

I wouldn’t want to be anywhere else
But here on this beautiful night;
In Petrudor I have made something
I can’t wait to show you, someday
