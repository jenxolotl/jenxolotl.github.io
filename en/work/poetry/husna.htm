Husna

How long has it been now
Since I heard the sound of your voice:
Perhaps two, three years blending
Into the slow solemn ache

Of the life I never got to know,
The life you never got to live

How I long to hear the women of Kabul
Speak and dance and sing again
And hope that when, if, I reach
The land of shattered dreams

Your smile won’t be hidden
