Willetton

The gentle wind lifts the trees
Like the sway of slowly waking giants

Resting on the green grass,
In city parks,

As the people come and go
In twos and threes,

Dogs astride, couples,
Children to their knees

The sky is blue and planes soar above,
Their wavering buzz in the song

Of many tongues together,
And the little singing birds,

Grocers, lawns, sweet gardens,
Plane flying overhead

The spaceship would have belied it,
This, a place on earth

The Willetton Spaceship was a prominent feature on the corner of Karel Avenue and the Roe Highway from the 1970s to the 1990s.
