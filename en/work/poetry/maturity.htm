Maturity

Is it maturity
To experience things fully
Instead of chasing the feeling
Of fleeting passions
Into dark corners
And desires past their due date;

Didn’t I learn
From the windowed slivers of light
In the ever flowing stream
That sadness, too
Can be quite bright
When you face it head-on,

Hurting without halting,
And feel the fresh air
Bursting again on your skin
On a cold winter morning;
The chill after the swim.
