Making Stirfry

Just drizzle out some oil
With one flick of your wrist
Turn the heat up to sizzling
Set your phone on the bench

Get your playlist cooking and
When the oil comes to boiling
Then get those onions frying
Toss the garlic and ginger

When it’s searing a little
And you feel it in your nostrils
It’s time to blend your style
While dancing wild in the kitchen

Diced carrot and chicken
And a hint of extra spice
When you’re in the rhythm
And you’ve come to realize

You’re the star again
And the planets still revolve
Around you, the whole universe
Your kitchen, everything

In tune

With the songs in your ears
Your creative vision, every colour
In order, the world upended
With a twist of your spatula

Feet stepping, both hands stirring
Four to the floor heart beating
Every move you make a sign
Of something great happening

Steam rising, sweat falling
They all want a taste
Of all that you’ve got
Goddess of the dance floor

Click click

The cameras come out
Like the glory days, it’s time
To try it and see
If you’ve still got the touch

The taste is right
The feel is right
The mood is right
The night is young

Prancing away like you’re a
Famous chef and rock star
Playing the performance
Of a lifetime, on the stage

Of dreams, working your magic
For the fans back home
They can always expect it
From you, making dinner

For the boy in the bath
You could forget for a moment
You were listening to ballads earlier
And your bills are piling higher

The stirfry’s ready, dig in
While it’s hot
