Content

True contentment lies not in gushing proclamations of love
Or the clinging to of hard-thought things

The opposite of negative rumination about the self
Is not a positive neuroticism, despite the feeling of the times,
But the negation thereof,

The comfortable disappearance of a warm rock under the shade
The roar of a four litre engine surging uphill
The honey birds in the background beating about the bushes.
