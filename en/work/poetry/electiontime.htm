Election Time

Beating out another sunrise
With the early morning crowd
Pre-emptively dishevelled, their
Clothes ill-fitting rounded out
Bodies, the colours ill-matching

The lines weathered into their
Faces before their years,
Their eyes downcast with weariness
Portending resignation to
Another day ahead where nothing

Will change, the managers will scream
And kids will cry and the homeless
Will yell incoherently across the street,
And the TV will repeat the same lines
From two parties on a TikTok video,

A thirty second clip enlarged to
A pixellated mess and repeated
Through days, through weeks
Until it loses all meaning except
A kind of high pitched white noise;

Everywhere the billboards proclaim
That in this election, this pivotal time
The police will need our back up
Knives and guns are apparently a crisis
But all I see around is stagnation
