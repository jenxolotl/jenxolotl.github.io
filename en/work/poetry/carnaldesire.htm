Carnal Desire

The longing
The wanting
The craving

Become things in themselves
To be desired and felt
Without

To look at the lives of others
The smiles and eyes
Stories

In tattooed arms, guitars
Creased books and
Car alarms

The swelling dam of feeling
Fills the heart and makes it
Empty

Let it beat
Let it flow
Let it fade

Into yellowed page on the bedside
And the dim grey light
Of a cloudy day.

The rain flutters gently
Steadily, and fills
The earth with life
