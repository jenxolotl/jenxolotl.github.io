Early Morning

Waltzing around in the darkness
I grasp at the old shadows
And faint outlines of things
I used to know

Like an aircraft on IFR,
I hold and circle the kitchen
Counter tops, fingers feeling
For the phone I placed down

A moment ago.
I’ve lost it many times here
And I’d use the light to find my way
Again, but the sudden strength

Hurts my eyes.
