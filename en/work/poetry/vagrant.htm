Vagrant

Man of the mountains
Man of the plains

Man of the forests
And the sprawling waves

That pass under the boat
Compressed and reshaped

From sunny Santa Catalina
To the vessels of my heart.

I feel every pulse and every beat
The fleeting warmth in wayward feet

That track across vast distant lands
Like a vagrant desert lizard

Never standing in one place.

I trace the steps of you,
Long travelling one

And linger in your prints
Your shadow, your smell

The shapes that remain
Long after you have gone

I would offer a home
Though there is none but the journey

And I will always be
A step behind
