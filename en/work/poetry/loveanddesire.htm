Love and Desire

Before I knew to desire
When the world was still

Myself, an inseparable projection

I took simple pleasure
In the presence all around

Me, the dust caught in a sandpit sunbeam

The earliest desire I remember
Is to be loved, perhaps

This is where it all went wrong
Love is not without me
