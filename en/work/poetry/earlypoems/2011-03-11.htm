2011-03-11

Do you remember?
Tears rolled down your cheek
Panic and confusion
Crowds of onlookers watching

The spectacle
The sound
The wail of the siren
The break in your voice
All became clear

Waves crashing
Against the wall
Couldn't be stopped
Or prevented
Water rushing down
Between gasps of breath

A sudden deluge
All channels flooded
Stony faced people watch
Dark grey days unfolded
And collapsed
Houses rolled along
Cardboard rubbish

Bodies in the water
Past the empty school
Lost
In the dark mass
Half-buried in mud
Broken cities and barren fields
Myriad prayers
Lighting up the starry web
Above tents and ruins

To be rebuilt
Restored
Poison air
Poisoned people
But not replaced
Hearts heavy with
What is gone

Across the great beyond
Rusted ships on sandy shores
And children's toys
Curios
Of the Pacific Coast Graveyard

Times gone by
Auld lang syne
The graduating class
Standing
With empty seats

Tears run down
In darkened rooms
Feelings washed away
With the waves
Flowers will bloom
We are here

Where all is gone
Smiling melancholically
Sun breaks through the clouds
Shining over open fields
Children playing and
Towns reborn

Five years ago
