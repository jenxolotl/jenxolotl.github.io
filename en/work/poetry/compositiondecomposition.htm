Composition/Decomposition

Composition

2023.
Clear blue sky
From the top of a dam
Wide open waters
A silky full moon
Pinned to the horizon

Decomposition

The year is not important
A severed kangaroo foot
Bone jutting out from glossy fur
Out back of a Kalamunda warehouse
Mud tyre tracks running through
