Trauma Bird

Pluck out my feathers one by one
Watch me scream in agony
As I’m violated over and over

Tears dripping on the concrete
Sadistic smiles all around
Pain still spinning in my mind

Cold steel around every corner
A barrier to my escape
I can’t find my way out

Vengeful eyes full of rape
Ready to take a piece of me
My small, quivering body

Mind shattered by what I’ve seen
Old trauma still repeating
Over and over, I want to fly

Far away from this place
To somewhere safe from myself
An island for my thoughts

If only my wings were stronger
I could go where I wanted
And leave my wounds behind
