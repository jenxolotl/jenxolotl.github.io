Moving

When I look at the old messages
And see the same words, and things
Crop up periodically in my life
I grow tired of the time weary minute hand

Making its way around the Sun
And wish for something new, something exciting,
Just the way I did ten years ago.

But where I used to think
It was the things themselves
That I wanted, now I know
It is the want itself
That I want, the desire to desire

And when I let this go
I can finally move unimpeded
