Birthday Present

To go a hundred miles
To give you space
Is as much an imposition
On you as myself

Everything else
Means nothing
If I am unable
To sit still;

When I bought you
A shell today
It was done joyously
Without worry:

In the happiness
of my unthinking
sincerity
