The Finest Mist of Rain

The finest mist of rain
dances in the sapphic blue
like a million tiny bugs,
their graceful spin halting and lifting
on the faintest gust of wind.

They fill this vast expanse,
still but for them and the frog
whose odd croak is the rhythm
to their erotic dance.

Their floating world free in frailty;
twists away and
vanishes in the blink of an eye.
