Marginalia

In the margins
The intervals
Of life between
The great unyielding machines
That drive us forward, farther
From our misty dreams

There lies the vision
Of alleyways unbounded
By regular scenes and
The limitations of
Shiny new things

Expanding
To fill the gap between our words.
