little weed

little trembly weed
out here shakin
quietly in your place
like a little boy
caught gently in the breeze

standing out in the cold
by the pool
when all the children are gone
you look so small down there

little leaves
frightened eyes
did a grown up leave you behind
where are your parents?

you know you’ll catch a cold
out here by yourself
in this paver jungle
with the pilferers and perverts
poking at you all round

yeah they’ll hack you down
in the jungles or the deserts
little weedy boi
but not me

I’ll look you right in the eyes and say
you can stay right here
in the asphalt playground
and maybe if you hang around a bit

you’ll end up like them
the men of heft and stature
hangin round the fence
tall and mighty

a public nuisance.
