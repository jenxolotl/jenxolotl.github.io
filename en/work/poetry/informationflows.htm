Information Flows

Information flows around me
Within me, throughout me
The little rock pool
By the side of the river

Where knowledge eddies
Redirect and swirl to stillness
Before being disturbed again
By a question and answer,

The flick of a hand raising
The alluvial silt and stirring it
To flow again, in new directions
The currents beyond my world
