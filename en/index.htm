Welcome to the official website of Jenxolotl!!!

I'm a poet, writer, photographer, and student of Asian languages and cultures, living in the seaside suburb of Rockingham, known to the Noongar as Mooriburdup, near Perth in the Southwest of Australia.This is my website and on these pages you can find examples of my work, bits about my life, ongoing projects, and other little things that catch my fancy.

Ngany kartidjiny moort Whadjuk wer Bindjareb Nyoongarang nidja Nyoongar Boodjariny baranginy. Ngany baalal nyittingal wer birdiya koora, yeyi, wer boordal kartidjiny, wer ngany boola-boola nyittingal wer birdiya Nyoongar Boodjarak nyinniny kartidjiny.

I acknowledge the Noongar Whadjuk and Bindjareb peoples as the traditional custodians of the land I work on. I acknowledge their elders past, present, and future, and acknowledge all elders of the Noongar Nation. [What's this?]
