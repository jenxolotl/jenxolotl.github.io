Graffiti of Rockingham

The Alleyway Murals

Scrooge McDuck mural, 2013

By Amuck Mason, Skotch Skore, and Ophoe Vodka

Looney Tunes Mural, 2013

By Sane Unique, Rare Chews, Wals Imps, Bec Madkat Neivey

These murals appeared together sometime in early 2013, and are well known to locals with their prominent location in the pedestrian alley behind Pegasus St. They are easily the highlight of Rockingham's street art scene, which unfortunately is hardly a significant achievement. As a Rockingham native, I've known them since they were new, though unfortunately I only have a photo of the Scrooge McDuck part in its pristine state. It can be seen that sometime in the nine years between it going up and my second lot of photos in 2022 the top-left corner of the mural was lost (I vaguely remember the bricks getting knocked off): in 2013 it said 'Electric Paint N' Brain Fluid.'
